Biography

Welcome! Glad to see you visit ;)

My name is Hengyue Liang, currently a 4th-year Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I'm grateful to be advised by Prof. Ju Sun.

Before coming to UMN, I have earned a Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering at Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden, and a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China.

Currently, I am most interested in application-driven deep learning and computer vision problems such as robustness, AI in medical imaging and healthcare. I am also avtively learning and looking for problems in human motion understanding and video analysis. Before joing Prof Sun's research group, I also had experience in robotics, system control and reinforcement learning.
